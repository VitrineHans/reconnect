The 24-Hour Loop
❓
QUESTION DROPS
New question surfaced to both friends. Category chosen by algorithm.
→
🎥
YOU ANSWER
Record a short video. Can't see friend's answer yet. Clock is ticking.
→
👀
BOTH ANSWERED
"Alex answered 👀" — push fires. Reveal unlocks. Watch together (or async).
→
🔥
STREAK +1
Streak grows. Video deleted after watch. New question in 24h.
⏰ If either person misses the 24h window — streak resets to 0. No exceptions. That's the stakes.

Where Monetization Lives
🎵
Sponsored Packs
Appear as a themed question category. "Spotify Wrapped: Your Friendship Edition." Users opt-in. Feels like a gift from the brand, not an ad.
🎁
Friendship Gifts
Surfaces at streak milestones (30, 60, 100 days) and after a particularly funny or emotional reveal. "Celebrate your 100-day streak — send Alex a coffee ☕"
🔬
Data Layer
Every question engagement, like/dislike, category preference, and completion rate builds the dataset. Anonymized trend reports: "What do Gen Z friendships talk about in 2026?"
