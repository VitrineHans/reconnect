What should Reconnect become?

Three strategic directions — each with a different growth model, monetization path, and addiction loop

🔥
The Intimacy Engine
Stay 1-on-1. Go deeper.

Deep Friendship App

Remain laser-focused on 1:1 connections. Win by being the best relationship-strengthening product on the planet. Think Duolingo for friendships.

Addiction loop

Daily streaks per friend · Question reveals · "Your friend answered!" push

Monetization

Premium subscription — streak shields, custom question packs, memory archive

Moat

Each friendship accumulates a shared history that's impossible to replicate elsewhere

🎮
The Social Game
Async party game. Viral by design.

Group Play Platform

Expand to squads of 3–8. Async video rounds, challenges, voting, and reactions. Think Jackbox meets BeReal — shareable moments, friend group energy.

Addiction loop

Group streaks · Round reveals · Reactions & voting · FOMO from group activity

Monetization

Freemium: free groups up to 4, paid for larger · Premium question packs · Game modes

Moat

Viral growth: every group invite brings new users; social pressure keeps everyone active

🌐
The Platform
Creator economy meets intimacy.

Question Marketplace

Open up question creation to the world. Creators, therapists, game designers build question packs. Reconnect becomes the engine powering them all.

Addiction loop

Fresh content always available · Creator-driven trends · Niche communities (couples, families, co-workers)

Monetization

Pack marketplace (30% cut) · Creator subscriptions · B2B licensing to therapy/HR apps

Moat

Content flywheel: more creators → more packs → more niches → more users → more creators
