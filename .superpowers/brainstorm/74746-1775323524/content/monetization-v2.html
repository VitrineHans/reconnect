Reconnect — Monetization Landscape

All models are free-to-user. Select the ones you want to build towards.

A

Sponsored Question Packs 🎵

Brands like Spotify, Airbnb, or Nike pay to sponsor themed question sets that appear as a special category. "Spotify: What song are you and your friend right now?" Feels like a premium content drop, not an ad. Brands pay per pack activation or per completed answer pair.

B

Friendship Gifts 🎁

At streak milestones (30, 60, 100 days) or after an emotional reveal, the app suggests sending a real-world gift — coffee via Uber Eats, flowers, an Airbnb experience. Reconnect earns affiliate commission (10–20%) on every completed purchase. Spending is entirely optional and always emotionally motivated.

C

Anonymized Trend Reports 🔬

Reconnect knows which questions spark the deepest engagement, which topics sustain friendships, and how connection patterns vary by age, region, and season — all aggregated, never individual. We sell this as research reports to:

  • Media & publishers — "What Gen Z talks about with their closest friends in 2026"
  • Brands — ad effectiveness and cultural trend data
  • Academic researchers — relationship science, social psychology
  • Therapists & coaches — which question types deepen bonds fastest

⚠ Zero personal data ever sold. Always aggregated & anonymized. Disclosed in onboarding. Opt-out available.

D

B2B Licensing — The Relationship Engine 🏢

License the core technology to businesses that want to deepen human connection in their own context:

  • Dating apps — use Reconnect post-match to move from texting to real intimacy
  • Couples therapists — assign questions as homework between sessions
  • Corporate HR — team culture and onboarding ("get to know your teammate")
  • Family apps — grandparents and grandkids, long-distance families

Priced per seat or per API call. Consumer app stays fully free.

E

Streak Gifting 🛡️

You can't buy streak protection for yourself — but you can buy a "Streak Shield" as a gift for your friend. If they miss a day, your gift saves the streak. The person giving pays; the person receiving gets it free. This keeps the product free while creating an emotionally resonant reason to spend money — you're protecting something you built together.

Small one-time purchase ($0.99–$2.99). High emotional value, low price resistance.

F

Annual "Friendship Wrapped" 🎬

Once a year (think Spotify Wrapped), Reconnect generates a shareable video recap of your friendship — highlights, streak stats, funniest moments, most revealing answers. The base version is free. A premium version with cinematic editing, music, and a printed photo book is a one-time purchase (~$9.99).

Also a massive organic marketing moment — users share their Wrapped on social, driving new signups.

G

Brand Moments & Seasonal Takeovers 🎄

Brands sponsor special limited-time events — Valentine's Day questions powered by a florist, a summer travel pack powered by Booking.com, a "New Year New You" pack by a wellness brand. Feels seasonal and cultural, not like advertising. Brands pay for exclusivity and reach during the event window.

Higher CPM than evergreen sponsored packs — scarcity and timing create premium pricing.
