What pulls users back every single day?

The primary addiction mechanic shapes everything — notifications, UX, and retention. Pick the one that feels truest to the product.

A

The Streak 🔥

The streak IS the product. Miss a day, lose everything. The anxiety of a number going to zero is one of the most powerful retention mechanics ever built — Duolingo proved it at scale. Each friendship has its own streak counter, making you responsible to your friend, not just yourself.

DAILY HOOK

"Your streak with Alex is at risk 🔥" → opens app → answers question → streak saved

GIFT TIE-IN

Milestone streaks (30, 100, 365 days) trigger gift suggestions

B

The Reveal Moment 🎭

The product is built around a single emotionally charged moment: you both answer in secret, then see each other's videos at the same time. The anticipation of what did they say? is the hook. You can't see their answer until you've given yours — which drives completion.

DAILY HOOK

"Alex answered the question 👀" → FOMO fires → you answer → reveal unlocks → delight/surprise

GIFT TIE-IN

Especially funny/deep reveals prompt "share this moment" → gift as celebration

C

The Friendship Story 📖

Every question answered adds a chapter to your shared history. The app builds a living timeline of your friendship — moments, milestones, inside jokes. The addiction comes from wanting to see it grow and from the fear of leaving gaps. Think of it as a scrapbook that builds itself.

DAILY HOOK

"On this day last year, you and Alex answered..." → nostalgia → opens app → continues the story

GIFT TIE-IN

Friendship anniversaries and milestone chapters are natural gift moments

These can coexist — but one should be the primary hook that everything else serves
